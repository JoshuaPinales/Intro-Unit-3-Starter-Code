You wake up in an unknown island. You don't know how you got there, but you see trees all around you.

The sun is bright and shining, but you feel a sense of danger when night will come later.

You decide to go the nearest tree and get some wood. You punch the tree and gather supplies to create tools and get minerals.

You got better tools and started farming for food by growing crops and plants.

You decide to climb the huge mountain and look around the area.

In the distance, you spot a big house near the forest.

You have managed to create yourself your own house! It has furniture, food, shelter, and most importantly, a soft, comfy bed.

You slept through the night and woke up safe and sound!

You decide to arm yourself up and fight the creatures of the night on your own. As you defeat each enemy, you grow more and more tired each time.

Your weapons break and you are left defenseless! Time to run!

You approach the house and knock on their door. The home owner greets you and welcomes you in.

He's an adventurer just like you! He gave you food and a place to sleep for the night. New friend acquired!

You decide to stay away from the house, it might be too suspicious!

As you wander around, night has already come and there are enemies all around! You then dug a hole and hide for the night. Not a bad home, I guess?
